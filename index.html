My name is Jae-Hyun Kim. I am a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Linguistics at Macquarie University in Australia.

I teach primarily into the Master of Speech and Language Pathology course at Macquarie University. I convene and teach into units about speech and language disorders in children.

I completed clinical training as a speech pathologist at the University of Auckland in New Zealand. I obtained my PhD also at the University of Auckland. My PhD was on phonological development in Korean-English bilingual children.

In research, I am primarily interested in speech and language production in bilingual speakers and speakers with speech and language disorders.
